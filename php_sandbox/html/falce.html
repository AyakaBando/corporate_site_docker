A Gentle Approach

falce - a gentle approach to office space division. Flowing shapes are combined with soft fabrics to produce expressive, comforting environments; and together with a range of partition shapes and components, falce provides you with a refined, elegant and flexible area management system.


Features


falce is available in five colour options for coordinating with your space: white, grey, orange, red and black. The fabric covers are also machine-washable for quick cleaning. The system consists of 4 round partition types, 2 flat types, and four height options for each respective type: 1.2m, 1.4m, 1.6m, and 2.1m. This gives the falce system the versatility to cater to a wide range of spaces and applications.

Assembly is simple, and can be completed in approximately 30 minutes. Each partition is also light and easily transportable, allowing for quick layout changes when necessary.


Optional Printing


Fabric printing is available optionally, allowing you to customise partition imagery to suit your needs.

 


METAPHYS Collaboration

METAPHYS is a collaborative design project created by one of Japan's leading designers, Chiaki Murata. The term "METAPHYS" is derived from "metaphysics", and connects elements of this scientific field with design and ergonomics. falce is one product from the METAPHYS range, and Morita Aluminum Industry is proud to be involved as the official producer and vendor of the falce system.


Variety of Applications


falce's white fabrics can be used as basic projection screens, making them ideal for simple home theatre applications or event space video displays.
